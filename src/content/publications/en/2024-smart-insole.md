abstract: "This study introduces a novel method of 3D human pose estimation using foot pressure data captured by a low-cost, high-resolution smart insole with over 600 pressure sensors per foot. Unlike prior carpet-type sensors, the wireless smart insole enables pose estimation regardless of location. Synchronized tactile and visual data (105,000+ frames, 5 participants, 7 actions) are collected. A deep neural network predicts 3D human poses using only foot pressure data, achieving 7.43 cm average localization error and 96.88% action classification accuracy."
